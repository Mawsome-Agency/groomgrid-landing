## #2 MoeGo — Best for Established Mobile Groomers

MoeGo is the market leader for mobile grooming software. It's a mature product with thousands of users, strong route optimization, and polished mobile apps.

What MoeGo does well:

**Route optimization:** The GPS-based routing is best-in-class. If you're a mobile groomer doing 8–10 appointments per day, MoeGo will shave 30–60 minutes off your daily driving time.

**Client management:** Detailed pet profiles, service history, and photo documentation are all here. The photo documentation feature is particularly strong — you can attach before-and-after photos to each appointment.

**Mobile apps:** Both iOS and Android apps are polished and reliable. You can check your calendar, send reminders, and process payments from your phone.

**Community and support:** MoeGo has the largest user community in the grooming software space. There are Facebook groups, Reddit discussions, and user-generated tutorials.

Where MoeGo falls short:

**Price:** At **$49/month** for the Basic plan, MoeGo is **$20/month more expensive** than GroomGrid at the entry tier. That's **$240/year more**.

**Route optimization pricing:** Here's the kicker — MoeGo's route optimization is locked behind the **$99/month Growth plan**. GroomGrid includes it in the $29 Solo plan.

**Pricing:**
- Basic (Mobile): **$49/month** — solo mobile groomers, basic scheduling, no route optimization

- Enterprise: Custom pricing — multi-location, advanced reporting

MoeGo is the safe choice if you want a proven platform with a large community. But if you're a solo groomer who doesn't need enterprise features, you're probably overpaying.

## #3 DaySmart Pet — Best for Multi-Location Operations

DaySmart Pet is the legacy enterprise player. It's been around for years, the learning curve is steep, and the pricing structure is complex.

DaySmart Pet isn't targeting solo groomers. It's designed for multi-location salons, franchises, and grooming businesses with 10+ employees.

What DaySmart does well:

**Multi-location management:** Manage multiple salons from one dashboard. View revenue across locations, transfer staff between sites, and run consolidated reports.

**Enterprise reporting:** Deep analytics on staff performance, revenue, and client retention. Track which groomers have the highest repeat client rate.

**Custom branding:** White-label options for franchises. The software can be rebranded with your company's logo and colors.

**Integrations:** Connects with accounting, CRM, and POS systems.

Where DaySmart falls short:

**Pricing opacity:** The starting price is around **$35/month**, but you need add-on modules for almost every useful feature. Each module adds **$25/month**.

**No AI:** This is a legacy platform without AI features like breed detection or smart routing.

**Overkill for small businesses:** If you're a solo groomer or run a single salon, DaySmart is like using a sledgehammer to hang a picture.

DaySmart Pet is the right choice if you're managing multiple locations and need enterprise-level reporting. For everyone else, it's too expensive, too complex, and missing AI features.

**Who DaySmart Pet is for:**
- Multi-location salon owners and franchise operators
- Businesses with 10+ employees
- Companies that need enterprise-level reporting and integrations

## FAQs About Pet Grooming Software

**What is the best pet grooming software for a solo groomer?**
GroomGrid Solo ($29/month) is the best choice for solo mobile groomers who want AI features at a budget price. It includes AI breed detection and route optimization that competitors lock behind premium tiers. If you prefer a proven platform without AI, MoeGo Basic ($49/month) is the mature alternative.

**How much does pet grooming software cost per month?**
Pet grooming software ranges from $20/month (Groomsoft) to $150+/month (enterprise plans). Most solo groomers pay $29–$49/month. Salons with multiple groomers typically pay $79–$99/month. Be aware of hidden fees: transaction processing (0.5–3% per payment), add-on modules (+$25/month each), and annual vs. monthly billing differences (annual saves 15–20%).

**Is there free pet grooming software?**
GrooMore offers a free tier with limited features. However, most free versions are severely restricted and don't include essential features like route optimization, AI breed detection, or robust payment processing. You'll likely need to upgrade within 1–2 months. The $20–$29/month range (Groomsoft, GroomGrid) offers significantly better value than free tiers.

**What software does MoeGo compete with?**
MoeGo's primary competitors are GroomGrid (AI-native, lower price), Pawfinity (premium positioning), and DaySmart Pet (enterprise focus). GroomGrid undercuts MoeGo by $20/month at the entry tier while adding AI breed detection that MoeGo lacks. Pawfinity competes on marketing features, while DaySmart targets multi-location operations.

**Can pet grooming software reduce no-shows?**
Yes — this is one of the most valuable features. Automated SMS and email reminders can reduce no-shows by 30–50%. If you charge $75 per appointment and prevent 2 no-shows per week, that's $600/month in saved revenue. Most platforms (GroomGrid, MoeGo, Pawfinity) include automated reminders.
